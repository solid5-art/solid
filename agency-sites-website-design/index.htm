Agency Web Design FAQs

How can we help you?

Do you have experience designing websites for other agencies?

Yes, plenty. We’ve built sites for brand strategists, PR firms, social media agencies and more. We’ve also partnered with agencies behind the scenes to deliver sites for their clients and leading platforms.

Can you work with our existing brand or help refresh it?

Yes, either. We can stick to your current brand identity or help evolve it as part of the project. It’s up to you.

How long does it take to design and build a new site?

Typically 6 to 12 weeks, depending on the size and complexity. That said, we’ve turned around sites in as little as 3 weeks when needed.

Will the site be easy for our team to update?

Yes, absolutely. We build with in-house teams in mind and provide training. Your team will be able to update content, add media and tweak things without needing to call us every time.

Can you help showcase our work and case studies effectively?

Yes, that’s something we’re really good at. We know how to make case studies and portfolios look great, feel interactive and actually get read.

What platform or CMS do you recommend for agency websites?

We usually recommend WordPress. It gives you full control, is SEO friendly and easy to update. We’ve worked with other platforms too, but WordPress tends to be the best fit for most agencies.

How do you approach SEO and performance in your builds?

All our sites are built with SEO best practices in place from the start. We can also review your current setup and put together an SEO plan as part of the project, then continue to support you after launch if needed.
