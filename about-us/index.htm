Our Values

Commitment

We support our clients through an ongoing partnership. We not only assist throughout the project but also provide continued aftercare and guidance. We remain actively involved, advising and collaborating with our clients to maximise their website’s long-term potential. We are the company that will stay late and go the extra mile to support you.

Honesty

In regards to the details of your project, we will always remain open and truthful when it comes to any design choices, and direction. We are here to bring our expertise to the table and produce the best possible results for our clients.

Evolving

Within our industry, changes happen fast and it has significantly evolved over the years. With this in mind, we are always eager to improve, constantly refining our design, UI/UX, development, technology, and interactivity to ensure we are delivering the best possible results for our clients.

We Care

We always aim to produce the best possible end product for our clients, often going above and beyond in our pursuit to create an exceptional end product. At the end of the day, we want to showcase the work we create, share it with the world, and take pride in it. This dedication is why many of our websites have won awards for their designs.

                                                          Junior Front-End Developer

                                                          • Semi Remote
                                                          • Development
                                                          Overview

                                                          The Role:
                                                          As a Junior Front-End Developer, you’ll bring designs to life by focusing on user experience, interactivity, and animation. While you don’t need to have extensive coding experience, a strong eye for design, creativity, and a willingness to learn are essential. This is a great opportunity to develop your coding skills while contributing to visually engaging and interactive web projects.

                                                          What We’re Looking For:

                                                          • A passion for design and interactivity – you enjoy creating things that feel alive!
                                                          • A basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript – advanced skills are a bonus but not required.
                                                          • Experience or interest in animation tools (e.g., GSAP, Lottie, or Spline) and creating smooth user interactions.
                                                          • A keen eye for detail and appreciation for good design.
                                                          • A portfolio showcasing your creativity – this could be personal projects, design work, or interactive experiments.
                                                          • An eagerness to learn and grow in a supportive, creative team environment.
                                                          • A good understanding of Adobe, Figma, or similar design tools is a bonus.

                                                          Key Tasks

                                                          What You’ll Be Doing:

                                                          • Collaborating with designers and developers to translate creative concepts into engaging, responsive web experiences.
                                                          • Implementing interactive elements and animations to elevate user experiences using libraries like GSAP or similar tools.
                                                          • Building and maintaining websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (basic knowledge required – we’ll help you grow!).
                                                          • Ensuring designs are implemented with pixel-perfect attention to detail.
                                                          • Testing and troubleshooting across browsers and devices to ensure compatibility and performance.
                                                          • Learning and contributing to the development of new ideas, techniques, and trends in design and front-end development.

                                                          Location
                                                          Semi Remote, Surrey
                                                          Salary
